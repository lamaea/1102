Research directions

This section is reserved for extended analysis: comparing robo-advisory platforms, regulatory considerations (e.g. suitability and disclosure), and extensions such as ESG preferences or tax-aware allocation. Content can be expanded as the project evolves.

Observations

Automated advice scales well to retail investors but must balance simplicity with transparency. Users benefit from clear explanations of how inputs map to portfolio weights—an area our Simulator demonstrates at a baseline level.
