Introduction

Conceptual Foundation

  • ● Autonomous Wealth Oversight: Harnessing digital contexts to provide financial advice through algorithmic models, which would greatly limit the need to have humans in the loop [6].
  • ● Strategic Translation: Turning multidimensional user preferences and risk appetites into a systematic, optimized portfolio with a calculation [9].
  • ● Market Inclusivity: A technological change that democratizes the high end of asset management, through the reduction of operational overhead and reducing barriers to entry into the retail game [2].

Primary Ambitions

  • ● Reducing Cognitive Biases: Disciplined data-driven decision making can be used to subordinate impulsive human feelings, avoiding irrational actions like market timing or panic selling [8].
  • ● Process Optimization: Optimization of the financial lifecycle through automation of all the processes including upstream profiling and ongoing optimization of asset weights [9]
